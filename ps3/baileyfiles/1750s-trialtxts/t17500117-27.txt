





140. 
               
                  
                     John 
                     Kible 
                  



               
             was indicted, for that 


               he, on the King's highway, on 
                     
                        Hannah, the wife of 
                              
                                 
                                    Geo 
                                    Warfoot 
                                 










                  , did make an assault, putting her in corporal fear and danger of her life, taking from her one linnen pocket, value 2 d. and 6 s. in monies numbered, the goods of the said George, against the will of the said Hannah
